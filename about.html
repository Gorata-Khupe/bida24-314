Who Are We?

Established in 2020, TSHWANTSHA Animation Studios (T.A.S) was founded by two young Botswana creatives who graduated with a Bachelor Degree in Creative Multimedia in 2017. Having worked in the industry, they saw a need for more vibrant, impactful creative content in advertising and media.

Our Mission

At TSHWANTSHA Animation Studio, our mission is to tell captivating stories through compelling animation. We aim to breathe life into ideas using creativity and cutting-edge animation techniques that elevate brand storytelling.

Our Story

Born from a passion for storytelling and visual arts, our studio is built on the creativity of animators, designers, and visionaries. Our work is rooted in innovation, and we continuously challenge ourselves to go beyond the ordinary.
